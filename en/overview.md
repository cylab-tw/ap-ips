## Asia Pacific Green Button
### One Click, Your IPS, Anywhere in Asia-Pacific
<div style="display: flex; align-items: center;">
  <div style="flex: 1;">
    <p>The AP Green Button is a patient-centered, standards-based digital access mechanism that enables individuals to securely access, download, and share their IPS across the Asia-Pacific region.</p>
    <p>It is designed to support trusted, cross-border health information exchange, empowering patients to authorize the use of their essential clinical data for care delivery, emergency treatment, and medication dispensing.</p>
    <p>The AP Green Button aligns with international interoperability principles promoted by WHO, HL7 International, IHE, and regional digital health initiatives. The AP Green Button is a one-click mechanism that allows patients to:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Download your IPS</li>
      <li>Store it in a Digital Health Wallet</li>
      <li>Share selected information via QR code or secure link</li>
      <li>Authorize access across countries, healthcare providers, and pharmacies</li>
    </ul>

### Policy Statement
* The AP Green Button promotes patient-mediated health information exchange by providing a simple, secure, and interoperable mechanism for individuals to access and share their IPS. By leveraging IPS, verifiable digital credentials, and selective disclosure technologies, the Green Button supports:
  - Continuity of care across borders
  - Emergency medical decision-making
  - Safe medication reconciliation
  -  Patient trust and data sovereignty
* The system is built on open standards and respects national digital identity frameworks, such as:
  - WHO Smart Health Cards
  - Japan My Number ecosystem
  - Taiwan Digital Identity Wallet

There are three common scenarios of AP Green Button: 
* Scenario 1: Cross-border Medical Care (Hospital)
* Scenario 2: Cross-border Pharmacy Dispensing
* Scenario 3: Emergency Care
