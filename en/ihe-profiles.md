## IHE Profiles
Relevant IHE profiles supporting document sharing, FHIR-based exchange, and cross-community access are introduced here. This section highlights how IHE profiles complement HL7 FHIR to enable robust interoperability.

### Data Content
* [International Patient Summary (IPS)](https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/fhir-ips)
* Sharing Value Sets, Concepts, Maps (SVCM)

### Communication Protocol
* Sharing of International Patient Summary (sIPS)
* [Mobile Health Document Sharing (MHDS)](#Mobile-Health-Document-Sharing-MHDS)
* Patient Master Identity Registry (PMIR)

### Privacy & Security
* Internet User Authorization (IUA)
* Audit Trail and Node Authentication (ATNA)
* [Privacy Consent on FHIR (PCF)](https://profiles.ihe.net/ITI/PCF/)
* Cross-Enterprise User Assertion (XUA)
